**Project Title**

A comprehensive business intelligence solution that leverages Zomato data to analyze market distribution, customer ratings, and pricing dynamics.
The insights support strategic planning, competitive positioning, and operational optimization

**Purpose**
The purpose of this project is to deliver actionable business insights from Zomato restaurant data using an interactive Power BI dashboard. It focuses on analyzing restaurant performance, customer ratings, pricing patterns, and service availability across different locations.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to identify market trends, compare restaurant categories, and evaluate customer preferences, supporting data-driven decision-making. This project demonstrates how business intelligence tools can transform raw data into strategic insights for market analysis and operational improvement.

**Tech Stack**
Power BI Desktop helps leadership access interactive, real-time insights through intuitive dashboards, improving the speed and quality of business decisions.
Power Query (ETL) – Ensures clean, reliable, and consistent data, reducing manual reporting effort and increasing confidence in analytical outcomes.
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Powers advanced KPIs and performance metrics, allowing stakeholders to track trends, compare scenarios, and measure business performance accurately.
Data Modeling – Creates a scalable and efficient data structure, supporting faster analysis and enabling future data expansion without redesign.
Interactive Visualizations (Slicers, Filters, Drill-through) – Allows decision-makers to explore insights dynamically, identify patterns quickly, and perform targeted analysis by location, pricing, or customer behavior.


**Data Source**
The dashboard is built on a consolidated Zomato restaurant dataset encompassing location, pricing, cuisine, customer ratings, and service attributes.
The data was standardized and validated through structured transformation processes to ensure reliability, consistency, and executive-level reporting readiness.

**Highlights**


**Executive-Ready Interactive Dashboard**
A fully interactive Power BI dashboard designed for quick executive consumption and deeper analytical exploration.
Clean layout with intuitive navigation to support both high-level overviews and detailed analysis.
**Market & Location Analysis**
City-wise and region-wise breakdown of restaurant distribution.
Identification of high-density markets and underserved locations to support expansion and market-entry decisions.
**Customer Ratings & Engagement Insights**
Analysis of restaurant ratings and voting patterns to understand customer satisfaction and engagement levels.
Enables identification of top-performing and underperforming restaurants.
**Pricing & Cost Analysis**
Evaluation of the cost for two across restaurants to understand pricing segmentation.
Supports comparison between affordable, mid-range, and premium dining segments.
**Cuisine & Category Insights**
Breakdown of popular cuisines and food categories across locations.
Helps identify customer preferences and demand trends in different markets.
**Service Availability Analysis**
Insights into online delivery and table booking adoption.
Evaluates the impact of service offerings on customer ratings and popularity.
**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
KPIs for total restaurants, average ratings, votes, and service availability.
Enables leadership to track performance at a glance.
**Advanced Filtering & Drill-Down**
Dynamic slicers for city, cuisine, rating, cost, and services.
Drill-through functionality allows targeted, granular analysis without clutter.
**Robust Data Preparation & Modeling**
Data cleaned and transformed using Power Query to ensure consistency.
Optimized data model for performance, scalability, and future enhancements.
**Business-Driven Insights**
Translates raw data into actionable insights that support strategic planning.
Designed to assist market analysis, competitive benchmarking, and decision-making.
**Portfolio & Enterprise Use Case**
Demonstrates end-to-end BI skills: data preparation, modeling, visualization, and storytelling.
Suitable for business stakeholders, analysts, and leadership presentations.
